Instant Ramen!

The Classic for the Poor!

Are YOU a poor college student with no money, no "time" to make real food when really you're just lazy to get up and make some food? Well oh boy do we have to introduce you to our lord and savior CUP-o-NOODLES!

The main ingredients in this dish are a simply de-hydrated noodles and maybe even chicken and veggies depending on the flavor you choose. Nonetheless, it's history is rich in Japanese culture (again!) as it was created by some 48 year old guy in Japan who saw people use coffee cups as ramen bowls.

Ingredients

  • Boiling water
  • Cup Ramen, Nissin or other brands, don't matter
  • If you have access to a microwave, use that instead of boiling the water if short on time

Steps

  1. Open up the cup ramen
  2. Pour boiling water into cup ramen until line
  3. Cover the top, and wait 3 minutes
  4. Grab a utensil and enjoy!
Instructions for Microwave:
  1. Open up the cup ramen
  2. Pour water into the cup ramen until line
  3. Place into microwave and microwave for 3 minutes
  4. Grab a utensil and enjoy!
